HOW TO ADD YOUR PROFILE PICTURE
================================

1. Find your profile picture on your computer
   (It can be .jpg, .jpeg, or .png format)

2. Copy your image file

3. Paste it into the "images" folder in this project:
   C:\Users\Yashu\porfolio website yashaswini\images\

4. Rename the file to exactly: profile.jpg
   (If your file is .png, rename it to profile.png and update index.html)

5. Refresh your browser to see the image!

ALTERNATIVE: If you want to use a different filename:
- Open index.html
- Find the line with: <img src="images/profile.jpg"
- Change "profile.jpg" to your image filename

SUPPORTED FORMATS: .jpg, .jpeg, .png
